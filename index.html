ABOUT US



ARTISTIC ELEMENT BRINGING INTO BRAND


Ráp Concept was established in Vietnam in 2019, is a brand of furniture and provides furniture design service.Our mission is to bring customers a new artistic perspective on elegance and ideal in minimalism, creating the pieces that upgrade and fit perfectly to your living space.



INTERIOR PRODUCTS FOR YOUR OWN MARK


Besides available designs from the Ráp team, we also provide interior design service according to your desire. Every person has a unique personality, and each designed product is to help you own personal traits, matching your demands and lifestyle



EVERY PRODUCT RESULTED FROM PROCESS


We - young Vietnamese, always desire to bring you high-quality products from a Vietnamese brand. Therefore, we focus on investing and taking care of every product. Each product goes through many stages and is entirely manual-produced, including drafting ideas, design, making samples, quality verification and delivering to the customers multiple-tested products. Besides, we choose plant-fiber concrete, one of the environmentally friendly materials, lightweight and durable over time, optimizing your using process






GEOMETRIC SHAPE ART



Exploiting the sharpness and weight from the basic shapes, our products are hand-molded from Vietnamese workers, bringing in their hearts to represent the systematic, minimalist but sophisticated way of thinking and the enthusiasm of youth as well.



BUILD CONNECTION BASED ON COMMITMENTS


Ráp Concept is built and developed based on commitment on product quality and best experience for customers. We closely associate in each stage, from product production to customer service. Ráp's commitment is an invisible line that connects and builds trust with you.



CUSTOMER EXPERIENCE



Value comprises art, and art comes from good manners and aesthetics. With this motto, we value aesthetics, focusing on the sophistication in every product detail to bring high value for your experience.
